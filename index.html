Malappuram Cancer Care Society is dedicated to providing comprehensive support and treatment to cancer patients.

About Us

The Malappuram Cancer Care Society (MCCS) is a charitable organization based in Kottakkal, Malappuram, Kerala, India, dedicated to supporting cancer patients and spreading awareness about cancer prevention. The society offers various services aimed at alleviating the burden of cancer on patients and their families, including:

Cancer Awareness Programs

Organizing events to educate the public about cancer prevention, early detection, and the importance of healthy lifestyles

Financial Support

Providing financial assistance to low-income cancer patients for treatments such as chemotherapy, radiation, and surgeries.

Medical Services

Facilitating cancer screenings and diagnostic camps in rural areas to promote early detection.

Rehabilitation Programs

Assisting in the rehabilitation of cancer survivors by helping them reintegrate into society.

Counseling and Support

Offering emotional, psychological counseling for patients and their families to help them cope with the challenges of cancer treatment.

Research and Advocacy

Raising awareness about the importance of cancer research in finding a cure.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive cancer care, offering compassionate support, advanced treatment, and personalized services to enhance the well-being of patients and their families. We are dedicated to improving lives through early detection, effective therapies, and holistic care, while fostering hope and promoting survivorship.

Our Vision

vision of the Malappuram Cancer Care Society (MCCS) is to create a cancer-free society by promoting awareness, ensuring early detection, and providing comprehensive care and support to cancer patients. They aim to improve the quality of life for those affected by cancer, especially the underprivileged, through accessible treatment, financial assistance, and community engagement. Their ultimate goal is to reduce cancer-related deaths and foster a healthier, more informed community

Services

Cancer care services encompass a broad range of medical and supportive care aimed at helping cancer patients through every stage of their journey, from diagnosis and treatment to recovery and palliative care.

These cancer care services aim to address the comprehensive needs of cancer patients, from clinical treatments to emotional and practical support, ensuring that they and their families are cared for throughout the process.

Treatment Services

Focuses on targeted therapies and personalized care.

Supportive, Palliative Care

Supportive care can be provided at any stage of cancer, from diagnosis to recovery.

Mental Health, Emotional Support

Counseling services for patients and their families to deal with the emotional toll of cancer.

Rehabilitation, Recovery Services

Helping cancer patients regain their strength, physical function, and quality of life after treatment.

Financial Support

Assistance with managing treatment costs, insurance, and financial aid options.

Caregiver Support Services

Resources and training to assist caregivers in providing proper care..
